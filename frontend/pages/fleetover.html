Platform Overview

AgriFleet simplifies fleet operations by integrating vehicles, drivers, trips, maintenance, invoices, and analytics into one secure platform. Built using modern web technologies, it improves operational efficiency, minimizes manual work, enhances transparency, and supports smarter business decisions through real-time insights.

Driving Digital Transformation

Our solution provides administrators with complete visibility over fleet activities. Users can assign drivers, schedule maintenance, monitor trips, generate invoices, and analyze operational performance through an intuitive dashboard.

Designed with scalability and enterprise-level security, AgriFleet ensures reliable data management and efficient fleet administration for agriculture, transportation, and logistics businesses.
